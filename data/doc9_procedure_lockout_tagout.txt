SAFETY PROCEDURE: SP-009
Title: Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) Procedure for Rotating Equipment
Applicable Areas: All pumps, motors, compressors, and conveyor drives.
Revision: 5
Effective Date: 1 January 2025

1. PURPOSE
To prevent unexpected energization or start-up of equipment during
maintenance or servicing.

2. PROCEDURE
2.1 Before any maintenance work on rotating equipment (pumps, motors,
compressors, conveyors), the equipment must be electrically isolated at
the source breaker.
2.2 A physical lock and tag must be applied by the technician performing
the work; only that technician may remove it.
2.3 Zero-energy verification must be performed by attempting a start
command at the local control panel before work begins.
2.4 For equipment with stored mechanical energy (e.g. tensioned belts,
elevated loads), additional mechanical restraint is required before LOTO
is considered complete.

3. RESPONSIBILITY
The technician performing the work is responsible for applying and
removing their own lock. Shift Supervisor must verify LOTO compliance
during routine shift rounds.
