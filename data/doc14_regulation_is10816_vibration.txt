REGULATORY REFERENCE EXCERPT
Standard: IS 10816 (Mechanical Vibration Evaluation of Machine Vibration by
Measurements on Non-Rotating Parts)
Relevant Clause Summary (for internal reference use):

For medium-sized rotating machinery (pumps, motors in the 15-300 kW range,
rigidly mounted), vibration severity is classified as follows:
- Good: below 2.8 mm/s RMS
- Acceptable: 2.8 to 4.5 mm/s RMS
- Unsatisfactory: 4.5 to 7.1 mm/s RMS, corrective action required
- Unacceptable: above 7.1 mm/s RMS, immediate shutdown recommended

Equipment operating in the "Unsatisfactory" band should be scheduled for
corrective maintenance within 30 days. Equipment in the "Unacceptable" band
should be taken out of service immediately pending investigation.

Internal facility policy sets the acceptable operating limit at 4.5 mm/s
for cooling water and utility pumps, consistent with this standard.
