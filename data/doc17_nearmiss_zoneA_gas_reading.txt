NEAR-MISS REPORT #NM-118
Date: 25 September 2025
Location: Zone A, Gas Manifold Area
Reported By: Shift Operator, Night Shift

DESCRIPTION:
During night shift rounds, a portable gas detector reading of 8% LEL was
recorded near the gas manifold, close to the permissible limit of 10% LEL
specified in SP-021. A hot work permit was active in an adjacent bay at
the time (approximately 12 meters away) for a pipe repair job. The reading
was logged but not immediately escalated to the Area Safety Officer, as
the operator judged it "still within limit." The hot work continued for
another 40 minutes before the shift log was reviewed and the readings
noted retrospectively.

CONTRIBUTING FACTORS:
1. Fixed gas detector GD-A-02 was out of service at the time (cross-ref
IR-0342), meaning only intermittent portable readings were being taken
instead of continuous monitoring.
2. No automatic escalation trigger exists for readings approaching (but
not exceeding) the 10% LEL threshold near active hot work.

CORRECTIVE ACTION RECOMMENDED:
Introduce a lower internal alert threshold (e.g. 6% LEL) that mandates
immediate hot work suspension and re-assessment, rather than waiting for
the full 10% LEL regulatory limit to be reached, particularly when fixed
monitoring coverage is degraded.

STATUS: Under review by Safety Committee.
