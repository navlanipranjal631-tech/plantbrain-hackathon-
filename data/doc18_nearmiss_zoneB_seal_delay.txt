NEAR-MISS REPORT #NM-095
Date: 20 June 2025
Location: Zone B, Cooling Tower Area
Reported By: R. Sharma, Technician

DESCRIPTION:
During routine inspection of Pump-204 (cross-ref WO-2118), minor oil
seepage was observed at the shaft seal and logged as "monitor, not yet
critical." No follow-up work order was automatically generated. The
seepage continued undetected as a priority item until it visibly worsened
three months later, resulting in an emergency shutdown and seal
replacement (cross-ref WO-2201).

CONTRIBUTING FACTORS:
1. No system exists to automatically flag "monitor" status items for
mandatory re-inspection within a defined window.
2. Reliance on the next scheduled quarterly inspection cycle (3 months)
allowed a degrading condition to go unaddressed for longer than necessary.

CORRECTIVE ACTION RECOMMENDED:
Any equipment condition logged as "monitor" during a work order should
automatically trigger a mandatory re-inspection task within 30 days,
independent of the regular maintenance schedule.

STATUS: Recommendation accepted, to be incorporated into maintenance
scheduling process from Q4 2025.
