REGULATORY REFERENCE EXCERPT
Standard: OISD-STD-105 (Work Permit System)
Relevant Clause Summary (for internal reference use):

1. No hot work permit shall be issued in an area where flammable gas
concentration has been recorded above 10% LEL within the preceding 4 hours,
until the area is re-tested and confirmed safe.
2. Confined space entry and hot work permits shall not be active
simultaneously within the same or adjacent equipment envelope, unless a
documented job safety analysis specifically authorizes the combination
with additional controls.
3. All permits must be countersigned by the Area Safety Officer and
displayed at the worksite for the duration of the activity.
4. Facilities must maintain a live permit register, cross-checked at
shift changeover, to prevent conflicting permits being active in
overlapping zones.

This standard forms the basis for facility-level procedures SP-014
(Confined Space Entry) and SP-021 (Hot Work Permit).
