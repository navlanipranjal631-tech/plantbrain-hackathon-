SAFETY PROCEDURE: SP-031
Title: Fixed and Portable Gas Detector Calibration Procedure
Applicable Areas: All zones with installed gas detection systems.
Revision: 2
Effective Date: 1 January 2025

1. PURPOSE
To ensure gas detectors provide accurate, reliable readings for early
warning of hazardous gas accumulation.

2. PROCEDURE
2.1 All fixed gas detectors must be calibrated at intervals not exceeding
90 days using certified calibration gas.
2.2 Portable gas detectors issued to personnel entering confined spaces
must be bump-tested before every use and fully calibrated monthly.
2.3 Any detector failing calibration must be immediately removed from
service and replaced with a calibrated unit; the affected zone must be
flagged as having reduced monitoring coverage until replacement occurs.
2.4 Calibration records must be logged and retained for a minimum of 3
years for regulatory audit purposes.

3. RESPONSIBILITY
Instrumentation Department is responsible for calibration scheduling and
execution. Area Safety Officer must be notified immediately if any zone's
detector coverage is reduced due to a failed unit awaiting replacement.
