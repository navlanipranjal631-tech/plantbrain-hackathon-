MAINTENANCE WORK ORDER #WO-2159
Equipment: Motor-11 (Conveyor Drive Motor, Zone C)
Date: 20 July 2025
Location: Zone C, Material Handling Area
Reported Issue: Unusual noise and intermittent tripping on overload relay.
Technician: S. Iyer
Action Taken: Winding insulation resistance tested, found within acceptable
range (450 MOhm, minimum acceptable 100 MOhm). Overload relay setting found
misconfigured at 85% of rated current instead of specified 100%. Relay
setting corrected. Coupling alignment checked and corrected using laser
alignment tool.
Status: Closed
